About


After years of holding various positions and analyzing her various strengths and weaknesses; Lauren Logan decided to go back to school to get her Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Design.

She has a very creative mind-set that is also very logical. In her opinion these attributes complement each other. Combining color, texture, music and technology in some way would be her ideal position, or any variation including any of these variables.

Her life experience often gives her a different perspective not always initially seen by others. In combination of being able to view things outside "the box", she is an asset to anyone who could utilize her many talents. She respects structure, rules and comprehends that the use of color, typography and hierarchy are important in graphic design.

She is an avid reader and enjoys traveling, as well as experiencing life. Some may call her an overachiever; but she thinks of it is more of finding her passion.
